The Maximum Order Quantity functionality in eCommerce is crucial for effective inventory management and ensuring fair distribution of products. By limiting the quantity customers can purchase, businesses can prevent stockouts, manage limited stock, and avoid bulk purchases by resellers. This functionality also controls promotional abuse, ensuring discounts benefit a larger customer base. It enhances customer satisfaction by maintaining product availability and promotes operational efficiency by managing manageable order sizes. Furthermore, it supports marketing strategies by creating urgency through scarcity, and helps comply with regulatory limits on certain products. Overall, this feature balances supply and demand, protects business interests, and improves the customer shopping experience.
